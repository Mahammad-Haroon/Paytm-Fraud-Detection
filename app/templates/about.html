About Us

Fraud in online transactions is a growing concern in the digital age. Fraudsters use various techniques to steal money or sensitive information, causing significant financial losses to individuals and businesses.

Our Paytm Fraud Detection System leverages machine learning to identify fraudulent transactions in real-time. The model analyzes transaction patterns, amounts, and other features to detect anomalies and flag potential fraud.

This system helps businesses and users protect their finances by providing an additional layer of security. By detecting fraud early, we can prevent losses and ensure a safer online transaction environment.
